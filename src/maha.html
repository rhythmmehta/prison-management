Maharashtra Prison Department comprises of 9 Central Jails, 28 District Jails, 5 Open Jails, 1 Open colony and 172 Sub-Jails. 
There are two separate Prisons for women at Pune and Mumbai. There is one Open prison for women at Pune. Maharashtra Prison Department 
has its Head Quarter at Pune and State Training Center at Yerawada, Pune 
The Prison Department works with Home Department of Government of Maharashtra.





At present construction of four jails at Sindhudurg, Jalna, Nandurbar and Gadchiroli is in progress and the work of constructing more 
barracks in the existing prisons has also been undertaken. Construction of new jails/barracks and repair of existing structures in jail 
premises is executed through Public Works Department. Funds are raised from State and Central governments. State Prison Department has 
also received funds from 13th finance commission for improving the existing facilities and amenities for the inmates of the prisons.
